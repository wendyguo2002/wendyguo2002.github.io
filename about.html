About Me

I am a Computer Science master's student at UC San Diego focused on building machine learning systems that bridge perception, simulation, and real-world applications. My work combines computer vision, deep learning, and system design to transform raw data into structured, actionable representations.

I am particularly interested in end-to-end ML systems, including data generation, model training, and deployment in interactive environments. My experience spans 3D motion reconstruction, real-time signal processing, and retrieval-augmented generation systems, where both algorithmic design and system integration are critical.

I enjoy working on problems that require connecting multiple domains — from low-level performance optimization to high-level ML pipelines — and building systems that are not only technically robust but also scalable and practical.
